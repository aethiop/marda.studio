Welcome to Marda Studios

We believe in giving users control over their platforms. We build decentralized applications that are owned and controlled by the people who use them.

  • The first and most important rule is that users must be in control. The platforms and applications we build must be owned and operated by the people who use them.
  • The second rule is that all data must be open and accessible. This is vitally important for ensuring transparency and accountability.
  • Finally, we must always remember that we are building this internet for the people, by the people. We must put the needs of the users first and always remember that technology is only a tool; it is the people who use it that make it great.
